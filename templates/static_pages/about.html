    About


    The name Kwippy is derived from the english word Quip.

    What?

    Kwippy is an easy and fun way to share your Gtalk, Yahoo and Facebook status messages with your friends and have interesting conversations over them. Kwippy offers a great platform for conversations by bringing out the best of forums, blogs and microblogs.

    Why?

    Statuses are omnipresent, from Instant messengers to social networks they are everywhere. They are a good measure of what you are upto, what you are feeling and what you are. Statuses give people reasons to talk. Status is the bridge between you, your friends and your potential friends. Read more about statuses in our blog post.

    How?

    Just add kwippy as a contact in your Instant messenger and voila your statuses start getting stored, you start getting instant message notifications whenever someone comments on your status, and a host of other functions.
